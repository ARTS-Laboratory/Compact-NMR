# Magnet Design

Designs for Magents following the nomenclature of:
* Letter - the style of the magnet
* Number - the nominal strength of the magnet in milliTesla (rounded to 10 milliTesla)
* Number - the nominal weight of the magnet in hunderds of grams 

For example, A650-10 is a magnet of design "A" with a nominal field strength of 650 milliTesla and an overall weight of 10 kg. The magnet designs are:
  * A - a dual permanent magnet arrangement with a 1018 steel yoke.
  * B - a 10-permanent magnet arrangement consisting of 2 primary magnets and 8 alignment magnets.
